Student Checklist

Fall 2021

Below you will find the steps you must complete prior to the start of classes. We have provided you with information as to which steps you have completed, and the ones that have yet to be completed. If you believe you have already provided the correct information or payment, or you have questions about the necessary requirements, please contact the appropriate office for additional information. Your prompt attention to these requirements will help you begin your semester as successfully as possible.

The following icons are used to indicate the status of the items below:

error - Item is Incomplete.

task_alt - Item is Complete.

announcement - This item contains information about an area, and offers you an option to make changes.

schedule - More information will be available about this item in the future.

  1. task_altEmail Account Password: Picked up. You may use your username and password to login to your Morrisville email using our webmail system. You will receive important information, including bill notifications, through your campus email account. This password is also used to access the Blackboard system and on campus network resources.
  2. task_altFAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid): Received.
  3. errorFinancial Aid Requirements: Please complete your unsatisfied requirements.
  4. task_altHealth Records: Received.
  5. task_altBilling: Semester Bill Payment Process Complete.
  6. access_time_filledClasses Scheduled: Scheduling is not yet available for Fall 2021.
  7. task_altEmergency Contacts & Preferred Contact Information: Provided.
  8. access_time_filledHousing Preferences: Please indicate your housing preferences using the Housing Preferences Form. After making your housing down payment, please wait 24 business hours before completing the form. If you wish to change your hall or room once you have been assigned, you must email reslife@morrisville.edu with your request, including your name and M# in the email. Incoming student requests are prioritized by date of housing down payment. Requests can be accommodated only if space is available and are not guaranteed. After August 1, no change requests are accommodated until 2 weeks after classes have begun.
  9. task_altHousing Assignment: You have been assigned housing. If you wish to view your assignment, you may view it at Housing, Roommate and Meal Plan Information.
  10. announcementMeal Plan: You may be interested in using the Meal Plan Selection and Change Form to change your meal plan.

Reminders:

  • Textbooks: Textbooks are not included on your bill or automatically ordered for you. You should plan on purchasing textbooks at the Campus Store when you arrive on campus.
  • The following link(s) will allow you to buy your books online from the Campus Store. When ordering your books please check to make sure all of your scheduled courses are listed on the page. If all your courses are not listed please contact the bookstore at (315) 684-6073. BUY YOUR BOOKS
