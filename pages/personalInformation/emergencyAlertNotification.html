Emergency Alert Notification

State University of New York Emergency Alert System

Our campus has implemented an Emergency Alert System based on the SUNY NY-ALERT service created and maintained by the New York State Emergency Management Office. This system will be used to provide information to our entire campus community in response to any event or situation on the campus which poses a serious safety concern. This system is one of a number of alert mechanisms which will be used during such an emergency but is the only alert system which sends alerts directly to you.

This system will only be used to send information regarding serious safety issues.

The SUNY NY-ALERT system is capable of sending e-mail messages, recorded voice messages and text messages to multiple addresses and phone numbers provided to the system. Participation is encouraged but is completely voluntary. It is provided to you at no cost (except for charges incurred receiving voice and text messages sent by the SUNY NY-ALERT system) and, only a single e-mail address is required to participate.
