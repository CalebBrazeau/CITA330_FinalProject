Welcome to Morrisville State College's ThinkPad University program!

With your Morrisville ThinkPad laptop, you will enjoy connecting to the Internet on a new campus state of the art 802.11ac
wireless network.

The college's technology initiatives prompted the New York Times to call Morrisville State College "a college at the forefront
of a technological revolution".

If you have any questions about your laptop order, please call the Morrisville Auxiliary Corporation (MAC) Technology Center
at 315.684.6422.


All laptop models include a carrying case, STOP theft tag, manufacturer's warranty and accidental damage protection*.

*The manufacturer's warranty and accidental damage protection coverage is from MAC's original date of purchase. The
accidental damage protection program excludes batteries and AC adapters. Laptop batteries are only warranted for a period
of one year from the original date of purchase. The approximate end date of the coverage is listed with each model.
