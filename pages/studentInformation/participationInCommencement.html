Participation in Commencement

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, 2020 Commencement ceremonies have been postponed from May and will take place later in the year, likely in the fall. The email originally planned for April 2 will be sent later, after clearer plans have taken shape. Students should still work with their school offices to confirm they have completed the proper requirements to graduate on time, regardless of the ceremonyâ€™s delay.


Congratulations! Commencement is a special time to celebrate your academic accomplishments. To ensure you have met the requirements to graduate and participate in 2020 Commencement, please work with your Dean's Office:

  • School of Agriculture, Business & Technology: Contact Dawn Morton-Miller, mortondr@morrisville.edu, or Susan Parkhurst, parkhusm@morrisville.edumortondr@morrisville.edu, or call 315.684.6056.
  • School of Liberal Arts, Sciences & Society: Contact Mary Hitchcock, hitchcma@morrisville.edu, or Jennifer Adams, adamsjl@morrisville.edu, or call 315.684.6081.
  • Nursing Department: Contact Andrea Mazor, mazorar@morrisville.edu or call 315.684.6016.

On Thursday, April 2, 2020, an email will be sent to your Morrisville email account that includes a link to the list of students who are expected to qualify to participate in 2020 Commencement exercises. The list will be categorized by school name, then by degree candidates for each program within that school. Please double-check that list to ensure your name is included, listed correctly, and you are set to participate in commencement-related activities.

Should you have any questions in regard to your graduation date, please contact your School Office listed above.

Should you have general questions in regard to commencement-related activities please visit the SUNY Morrisville Commencement webpage.
